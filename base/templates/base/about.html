About

YXE Food Bank is one of the regional food banks serving Saskatoon. Through YXE Food Bank's network of food distribution facilities, we are able to meet our customers' basic nutritional needs. More than 100 agencies, such as soup kitchens, homeless shelters, and religious institutions, are part of our network. YXE Food Bank strives to uphold the dignity of those we assist while also protecting their health by delivering healthy, culturally appropriate food to those in need where it is most needed.

If you have some questions, feel free to contact us.
